Did Danielle take John's last name?

Yes, but we aren't changing it on the website or wedding materials because we consider this reception to be part of the joining of our two families. For example, our room block and signange will use Millett/Migliozzi.


Will I need to wear a mask?

The vast majority of our guests are vaccinated, but we understand that there is still a risk associated with breakthrough COVID cases. Masks are not required, but are certainly welcome.
