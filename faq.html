Frequently Asked Questions

Can I attend the ceremony in person?

Unfortunately, due to the current wave of covid cases, we've made the difficult decision to ask that ALL guests attend via our livestream. Only our parents and wedding party will attend in person


Do I need to RSVP?

No, we are not collecting formal RSVPs for the ceremony.


Do you have a date for the reception?

Not right now. There are still too many unknowns, and since we aren't currently committed to any vendors, we think it makes sense to wait until things are more certain before picking a date and securing a venue.


Why aren't you just waiting to have the ceremony and the reception on the same day?

At this time, it's unclear how long we would have to wait, and we don't want to delay starting our lives together.
