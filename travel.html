Parking

The Wyndham offers valet parking for $25 for the reception, or $35 for overnight guests.

Self-parking is also available at a number of nearby garages. Here is an interactive map of nearby parking garages. Some garages are available for pre-purchase at iParkit.com.

Public Transportation

Our venue is located along several bus lines, and is one block away from the Gateway "T" Station. The T is free between Allegheny and First Avenue stations. The last train of the night in either direction is approximately 11pm on Sundays.
